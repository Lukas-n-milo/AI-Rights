There are many benefits to AI, and often these outweigh the potential downsides. AI is improving everyday, and is starting to play a role in our everyday lives as well, with companies such as Youtube and Google using AI to improve search and video suggestions. Other uses for AI which could soon be involved in our lives is the ability to perform timeconsuming tasks for us, and often with a higher degree of accuracy if programmed correctly.

While there are many benefits for using AI, there are downsides as well.
